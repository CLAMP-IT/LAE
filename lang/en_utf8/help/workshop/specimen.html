Specimen Assessment Form

This page shows the details of the actual form used to grade your assignment. It will be used by the teacher to grade your work. Further, in a peer graded assignment, the same form will be used by yourself and your fellow students to grade the submitted pieces of work.

Please note that although you can change the grades on this form these changes are NOT saved. This is simply a specimen form, but a very similar form will be used by the teacher and possibly by you during this assignment.
